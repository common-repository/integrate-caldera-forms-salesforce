Caldera Forms Salesforce Integration

== Description ==

The integration of Caldera Forms and Salesforce plugin lets you add a new Salesforce Processor to Caldera form. It automatically syncs data from your Caldera form to your Salesforce CRM when the form is submitted.

- Easily collect data and push to Salesforce

== Installation ==
1. Login to your WordPress dashboard and navigate to Plugins > Add New
2. Search for "Integration of Caldera Forms and Salesforce".
3. Click install.
4. Click activate.
